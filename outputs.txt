INPUT: Explain what a neural network is in simple terms.

OUTPUT:
A neural network is a type of computer system inspired by the human brain. It consists of layers of connected nodes (like neurons) that process information and learn patterns from data. Neural networks are often used for tasks like image recognition, language processing, and predictions.
